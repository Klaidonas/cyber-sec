Elements of a wireless network

	IoT - internet of things. Pvz:
		išmanieji sriubrliai, išmanieji šaldytuvai, sensoriai...

	Wireless hosts :
		Laptop, smartphone, IoT ;
		Run applications;
		May be stationary (non-mobile) or mobile;
		**Wireless does not always mean mobility!**
		
	Ad hoc mode - bevielio tinklo setup'o būdas, kai kiekvienas įrenginys yra tiesiogiai sujungtas su kitu įrenginiu nenaudojant centralizuoto routerio ar AP(access point).
		Kiekvienas įrenginys(node'as) gali siųsti duomenis kitam artimam įrenginiui ir kartu jie suformuoja savarankiškai organizuojamą tinklą(self-organizing network).
	Ad hoc mode:
		No base stations;
		Nos can only transmit to other nodes within linkcoverage;
		Nodes organize themselves into a network: route among themselves
		
		
802.11: KANALAI (Channels, association)

	2.4GHz kanalai yra atskirti ir 5Ghz kanalai atskirti
	
	
Personl area networks: Bluetooth:
	less than 10m diameter;
	Replacement for cables(mouse, keyboard, headphones);
	Ad hoc: no infrastructure;
	2.4-2.5 GHz ISM radio band, up to 3 Mbps;
	Master controller / clients devices:
		master polls clients, grants requests for client transmissions.
